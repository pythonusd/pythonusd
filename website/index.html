Python USD

Lamden's first open source, smart contract controlled stablecoin

Python USD (PUSD) is an algorithmic stablecoin pegged to the US dollar and collateralized by TAU.
All operations involving PUSD, such as issuance and payouts, are fully transparent and governed by a smart contract.
